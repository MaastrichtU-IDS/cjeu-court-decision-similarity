61998OOrder of the Court (First Chamber) of November 1998.  -  Anne-Marie Toller v Commission of the European Communities.  -  Appeal clearly inadmissible and clearly unfounded.  -  Case C-149/P.  
European Court reports Page I-Summary
Keywords
Appeals - Grounds - Erroneous assessment of the facts - Inadmissibility (EC Treaty, Art 168a; EC Statute of the Court of Justice, Art. 51) 
Summary
Under Article 168a of the Treaty and Article of the Statute of the Court of Justice, an appeal may be based only on grounds relating to the infringement of rules of law, to the exclusion of any appraisal of the facts.
