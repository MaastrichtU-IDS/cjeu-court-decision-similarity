
Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of February – Commission v Spain
(Case C-546/03) 
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Communities’ own resources – Community Customs Code – Procedures for collecting import or export duties – Late payment of own resources relating to those duties and failure to pay default interest
Communities’ own resources – Establishment and making available by Member States (Council Regulations Nos 1552/89, Art. 11, 1854/89, Art. 5, 1150/2000,
         Art. 1,and 2913/92, Art. 220(1)) (see paras 28, 32, 35, operative part)
      
Re:
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Article of Council Regulation (EEC) No 2913/of October establishing
                  the Community Customs Code (OJ L 302, p. 1) and Article of Council Regulation (EEC) No 1854/of June on the
                  entry in the accounts and terms of payment of the amounts of the import duties or export duties resulting from a customs debt
                  (OJ L 186, p. 1) – Late payment of part of the European Communities’ own resources in the case of late recovery of customs
                  duty – Refusal to pay default interest due as a consequence of the delay in making the entry in the Commission’s account
               
Operative part:
The Court:
1.
Declares that:
(a)      by failing to comply with the time-limits for late entry in the accounts of duties arising from a customs debt laid down in
         Article of Council Regulation (EEC) No 1854/of June on the entry in the accounts and terms of payment of the
         amounts of the import duties or export duties resulting from a customs debt, and, from January 1994, with Article 220(1)
         of Council Regulation (EEC) No 2913/of October establishing the Community Customs Code, leading to a delay in making
         available of own resources, and 
      
(b)      by failing to pay to the Commission of the European Communities the interest under Article of Council Regulation (EEC,
         Euratom) No 1552/of May implementing Decision 88/376/EEC, Euratom on the system of the Communities’ own resources,
         and, from May of Article of Council Regulation (EC, Euratom) No 1150/of May implementing Decision
         94/728/EC, Euratom on the system of the Communities’ own resources, 
      
the Kingdom of Spain has failed to fulfil its obligations under all of those provisions;
2.
Orders the Kingdom of Spain to pay the costs;
3.
