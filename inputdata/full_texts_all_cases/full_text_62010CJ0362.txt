
Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of October – Commission v Poland
(Case C-362/10)
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Directive 2003/98/EC – Re-use of public sector information – Incorrect transposition of or failure to transpose certain articles within the period prescribed
1.                     Acts of the institutions – Directives – Implementation by Member States – Transposition of a directive without legislative
            action – Conditions – Existence of a general legal context ensuring the full application of the directive (Art. 288, third
            para., TFEU; European Parliament and Council Directive 2003/98) (see paras 46, 60)
2.                     Acts of the institutions – Directives – Implementation by Member States – Directive intended to create rights for individuals
            – Requirements of clarity and legal certainty – Directive 2003/– Right to re-use public sector information – No transposition
            by statutory or regulatory provision – Not permissible (Art. 288, third para., TFEU; European Parliament and Council Directive
            2003/98, Art. 2, paras to 3, 4, 6, and 10) (see paras 50-52, 54-56)
3.                     Acts of the institutions – Directives – Implementation by Member States – Transposition of a directive without legislative
            action – Not permissible in the case of an express requirement for reference to be made to a directive (Art. 288, third para.,
            TFEU; European Parliament and Council Directive 2003/98) (see para. 58)
4.                     Acts of the institutions – Directives – Implementation by Member States – Necessity of full transposition into national law
            – Activity referred to by a directive does not exist in a Member State – No effect (Art. 288, third para, TFEU; European Parliament
            and Council Directive 2003/98) (see para. 64)
Re: 
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Failure to adopt, within the prescribed period, the measures necessary to
                  comply with Articles 2, 3, 4, 6, 7, 8, and of Directive 2003/98/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of
                  November on the re-use of public sector information (OJ L 345, p. 90).
               
Operative part
The Court: 
1.
	Declares that, by failing to adopt, within the prescribed period, all the laws, regulations and administrative provisions
                     necessary to transpose into the Polish legal order Articles to 4, to 8, and of Directive 2003/98/EC of the European
                     Parliament and of the Council of November on the re-use of public sector information, the Republic of Poland has failed
                     to fulfil its obligations under those articles; 
                  
2.
	Orders the Republic of Poland to pay the costs.
