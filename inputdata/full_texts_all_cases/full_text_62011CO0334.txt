
Order of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of March —Lancôme v OHIM(Case C‑334/P)

Appeal against the judgment of the General Court (Eighth Chamber) of April in Case T‑466/Lancôme 
v 
OHIM
 by which that court dismissed an action for annulment brought by the applicant for the word mark ‘ACNO FOCUS’ for goods in

1.
	The appeal is dismissed.
