
Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of March – K & L Ruppert Stiftung v OHIM
(Case C‑90/P)
Appeal – Community trade mark – Application for registration of the Community figurative mark CORPO LIVRE – Opposition on the part of the proprietor of the earlier national and international word marks LIVRE – Proof of use of the earlier marks submitted out of time – Rejection of the opposition
1.                     Community trade mark – Procedural provisions – Time-limits (Commission Regulation No 2868/95, Art. 71(1)) (see paras 21-24)
2.                     Community trade mark – Procedural provisions – Opposition proceedings (Council Regulation No 40/94, Arts 43(2) and (3), and
            74(2); Commission Regulation No 2868/95, Rule 22(1)) (see paras 35-36)
Re: 
Appeal against the judgment of the Court of First Instance (Second Chamber) in Case T-86/K & L Ruppert Stiftung
 v 
OHIM – Lopes de Almeida Cunha and Others
 by which that Court dismissed an action for annulment by the proprietor of the national and international word marks ‘LIVRE’
                  for goods in Class against the decision of the First Board of Appeal of OHIM of December rejecting the appeal against
                  the decision of the Opposition Division refusing the opposition filed against the registration of the figurative Community
                  trade mark ‘CORPO LIVRE’ for goods in Classes and – Opposition proceedings – Rejection of the opposition on the ground
                  of late submission of proof of use of earlier marks.
               
Operative part 
The Court:
1.
	Dismisses the appeal;
2.
	Orders K & L Ruppert Stiftung & Co. Handels-KG to pay the costs.
