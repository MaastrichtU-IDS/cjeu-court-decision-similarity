
Order of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of February – Deutsche Post and DHL Express (formerly DHL International) v Commission
(Case C-367/P)
Appeal – Aid granted by the Italian authorities to Poste Italiane)
1.                     Actions for annulment – Natural or legal persons – Measures of direct and individual concern to them (Arts 87(1) EC, 88(2)
            EC, and 230, fourth para., EC) (see  paras 37-38, 40-42, 50-51)
2.                     Appeals – Grounds – Review by the Court of the assessment of the evidence – Possible only where the clear sense of the evidence
            has been distorted (Art. EC; Statute of the Court of Justice, Art. 58) (see para. 43)
Re:
Appeal against the order of the Court of First Instance (Second Chamber, Extended Composition) of May in Case T-358/Deutsche Post and DHL International 
v 
Commission
 dismissing as inadmissible the action for annulment of Commission Decision 2002/782/EC of March on the aid granted
                  by Italy to Poste Italiane SpA (formerly Ente Poste Italiane) (OJ L 282, p. 29)
               
Operative part:
The Court:
1.
Dismisses the appeal;
2.
Orders Deutsche Post AG and DHL Express (Italy) Srl, formerly DHL International Srl, to pay the costs;
3.
Orders the Italian Republic to bear its own costs.
