
ORDER OF THE COURT (Fifth Chamber) 
October Case C-238/P
Derya Beyatli
v
Commission of the European Communities 
(Appeals – Civil service – Open competition for citizens of the Republic of Cyprus – Notice of competition – Time-limits – Complaint – Letter sent to the Head of the Commission Delegation to Cyprus)
Appeal: against the order of the Court of First Instance (Fifth Chamber) of March in Case T-455/Beyatli and Candan v Commission [2007] ECR-SC I-A-2-and II-A-2-by which the Court of First Instance dismissed as inadmissible the application by
         the applicants seeking the annulment of the decision of the selection board in Competition EPSO/A/1/for the establishment
         of a reserve list of assistant administrators of Cypriot nationality not to admit them to the oral tests in that competition.
      
Held: The appeal is dismissed. Mrs Beyatli is ordered to pay the costs.
      
Summary
Appeals – Grounds – Plea alleging failure to produce documents – Documents not relevant for ruling on the admissibility of
            the action – Appeal dismissed
