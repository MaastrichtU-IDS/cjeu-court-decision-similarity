Grounds
BY LETTER LODGED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON JUNE THE APPLICANTS INFORMED THE COURT, IN ACCORDANCE WITH ARTICLE OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE, OF THEIR WISH TO DISCONTINUE THE PROCEEDINGS .  BY LETTER LODGED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON JUNE THE DEFENDANT TOOK FORMAL NOTE OF THE DISCONTINUANCE .  PURSUANT TO ARTICLE ( ) OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE IN CONJUNCTION WITH ARTICLE THEREOF, IT IS APPROPRIATE TO ORDER THE PARTIES TO BEAR THEIR OWN COSTS .  
Operative part
ON THOSE GROUNDS,  THE COURT ( FIRST CHAMBER )  HEREBY ORDERS AS FOLLOWS :  ( ) CASE 323/SHALL BE REMOVED FROM THE REGISTER OF THE COURT;  ( ) THE PARTIES SHALL BEAR THEIR OWN COSTS .  LUXEMBOURG, JULY .
