
Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of February – Adriatica di Navigazione v Commission
(Case C-111/P)
Appeal – Article 85(1) of the EC Treaty (now Article 81(1) EC) – Competition – Cartels – Agreement between undertakings – Proof of participation of an undertaking in a cartel
1.                     Competition – Agreements, decisions and concerted practices – Definition of the market – Subject-matter (EC Treaty, Art. 85(1)
            (now Art. 81(1) EC)) (see paras 30-31)
2.                     Competition – Agreements, decisions and concerted practices – Participation of an undertaking in an anti-competitive initiative
            (EC Treaty, Art. 85(1) (now Art. 81(1) EC)) (see para. 48)
3.                     Appeals – Grounds of appeal – Mistaken assessment of the facts – Inadmissibility – Review by the Court of Justice of the assessment
            of the evidence – Excluded unless the clear sense of the evidence has been distorted (Art. EC; Statute of the Court of
            Justice, Art. 58) (see paras 49, 62)
4.                     Competition – Fines – Amount – Reduction in fine in exchange for cooperation (Council Regulation No 17, Art. 15) (see para.
            91)
Re:
Appeal against the judgment of the Court of First Instance (Fifth Chamber) of December in Case T-61/Adriatica di Navigazione 
v 
Commission
, dismissing as unfounded an action for the annulment of the Commission decision of December relating to a proceeding
                  under Article of the EC Treaty (IV/– Greek Ferry Boats)
               
Operative part:
The Court:
1.
Dismisses the appeal;
2.
Dismisses the cross-appeal of the Commission of the European Communities;
3.
Orders Adriatica di Navigazione SpA to pay 90% of the costs;
4.
Orders the Commission of the European Communities to pay 10% of the costs.
