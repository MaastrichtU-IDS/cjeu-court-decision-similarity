
Order of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of March – Baumann
(Case C-309/INT)
Procedure – Application for interpretation – Inadmissibility
Procedure – Interpretation of a judgment – Application for interpretation of a preliminary ruling – Inadmissibility (Statute of the Court of Justice, Art. 43; Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice, Art. 92(1)) (see paras
         5, 8)
      
Re:
Application for interpretation of a preliminary ruling given by the Court of Justice, on March 2009, in Case C-309/Baumann
.
Operative part
1.
	The application for interpretation is dismissed. 
2.
	Baumann GmbH is ordered to bear its own costs.
