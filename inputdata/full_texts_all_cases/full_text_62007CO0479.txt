
Order of the President of the Court of February – France v Council
(Case C‑479/R)
Applications for interim measures – Application for suspension of operation of a measure – Fisheries policy – Regulation No 809/– Concept of ‘drift-net’ – Whether ‘thonaille’ nets included – Urgency
1.                     Applications for interim measures – Suspension of operation of a measure – Conditions for granting – Prima facie case – Urgency
            – Serious and irreparable damage – Cumulative nature – Weighing-up of all the interests at stake (Art. EC) (see paras
            16-17)
2.                     Applications for interim measures – Suspension of operation of a measure – Conditions for granting – Urgency – Serious and
            irreparable damage – Burden of proof (Art. EC) (see paras 18-19)
3.                     Applications for interim measures – Suspension of operation of a measure – Suspension of operation of Regulation No 809/containing a definition of the concept of ‘drift-net’ capable of requiring an effective cessation of fishing with ‘thonaille’
            nets – Conditions for granting – Urgency – Serious and irreparable damage – Financial loss (Art. EC; Council Regulation
            No 809/2007) (see paras 24-25, 28-31)
Re: 
Action for annulment – Annulment of Council Regulation (EC) No 809/of June amending Regulations (EC) No 894/97,
                  (EC) No 812/and (EC) No 2187/as concerns drift nets (OJ L 182, p. 1) – Concept of ‘drift nets’ – Inclusion
                  in that concept of stabilised nets such as the ‘thonaille’ – Infringement of the obligation to provide reasons and of the
                  principles of proportionality and non-discrimination.
               
Operative part
The Court: 
1.
	Rejects the application for interim measures;
2.
	Reserves the costs.
