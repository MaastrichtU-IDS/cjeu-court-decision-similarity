
Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of April – TEA-CEGOS and STG v Commission
(Case C‑189/P)
Appeal – Public contracts for services – Call for tenders relating to a multiple framework contract for the short term recruitment of experts responsible for providing
         technical assistance to third countries – Rejection of the appellants’ tender – Appeal manifestly unfounded
      
European Communities’ public procurement – Tendering procedure (see paras 29-31)
Re: 
Appeal brought against the judgment of the Court of First Instance (Second Chamber) of February in Joined Cases T-376/and T-383/TEA-CEGOS and Others 
v 
Commission
 dismissing the application for annulment, first, of the Commission’s decisions of October rejecting the tenders submitted
                  by the applicants in the context of the procedure for the call for tenders bearing the reference ‘EuropeAid/119860/C/SV/multi-Lot
                  7’ and, second, of any other decision taken by the Commission in the context of the same call for tenders following the decisions
                  of October 2005.
               
Operative part
The Court: 
 
Dismisses the appeal;
 
Orders TEA-CEGOS SA and Services techniques globaux (STG) SA to pay the costs.
