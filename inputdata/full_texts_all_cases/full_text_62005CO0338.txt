
Order of the Court (Second Chamber) of July – Front national and Others v Parliament and Council
(Case C-338/P)
Appeal – Regulation (EC) No 2004/– Regulation and funding of political parties at European level – Action for annulment – Objection of inadmissibility – Act open to challenge – Standing to bring proceedings – Inadmissibility – Appeal manifestly inadmissible
Appeal – Grounds – Mere repetition of the pleas and arguments put forward before the Court of First Instance – Failure to specify the error of law relied on – Inadmissible (Art. EC; Statute of the Court of Justice, Art. 58, first para.; Rules of Procedure of the Court, Art. 112(1)(c),
         first subpara.) (see paras 23-25)
      
Re: 
APPEAL brought against the judgment of the Court of First Instance (Second Chamber) of July in Case T-17/Front national and Others
 v 
Parliament and Council
, by which it dismissed as inadmissible an action for the annulment of Regulation (EC) No 2004/of the European Parliament
                  and of the Council of November on the regulations governing political parties at European level and the rules regarding
                  their funding (OJ L 297, p. 1).
               
Operative part:
The Court:
 
Dismisses the appeal;
 
Orders the appellants to pay the costs.
