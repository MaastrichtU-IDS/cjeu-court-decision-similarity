
Order of the Court (Second Chamber) of May – Petrides v Commission
(Case C‑64/P-REV)
Application for revision – Rules of the Court of Justice governing languages – Manifest inadmissibility
1.                     Procedure – Revision of a judgment – Conditions for the admissibility thereof – New fact – Meaning (Statute of the Court of
            Justice, Art. 44) (see para 12)
2.                     Procedure – Rules on languages – Choice of the language of the case ‑ Obligation to respect choice in the case of an appeal
            or application for revision (Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice, Arts 29(3) and 100(3)) (see paras 13-15)
Re: 
Application for revision of the judgment of the Court of Justice (Third Chamber) of September in Case C-64/P by
                  which that court dismissed the appeal brought against the judgment of the Court of First Instance (Fourth Chamber) of December
                  in Case T-152/– Rules of the Court governing languages.
               
Operative part:
The Court: 
1.
	Dismisses the application for revision;
2.
	Orders Odette Nicos Petrides Co. Inc. to bear its own costs.
