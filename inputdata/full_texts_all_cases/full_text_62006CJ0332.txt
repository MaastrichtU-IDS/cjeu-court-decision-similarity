
Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of October – Greece v Commission
(Case C‑332/P)
Appeal – EAGGF – Exclusion of expenditure from financing owing to failure to comply with Community rules
1.                     Agriculture – EAGGF – Clearance of accounts (Council Regulation No 729/70, as amended by Regulation No 1287/95, Art. 5(2)(c),
            fifth para., and No 1258/1999, Art. 7(4)(a); Commission Regulation No 1663/95, as amended by Regulation No 2245/1999, Art.
            8(1), first para.) (see paras 46-48, 52)
2.                     Appeals – Grounds – Review by the Court of the legal classification of the facts – Admissibility (Art. EC) (see para.
            59)
Re: 
Appeal brought against the judgment of the Court of First Instance (Second Chamber) of June in Case T-251/Greece
 v 
Commission
 by which the Court dismissed an action seeking annulment of Commission Decision 2004/457/EC of April excluding from
                  Community financing certain expenditure incurred by the Member States under the Guarantee Section of the European Agricultural
                  Guidance and Guarantee Fund (EAGGF) (notified under document number C(2004) 1706) (OJ L 156, p. 48).
               
Operative part:
The Court: 
 
Dismisses the appeal;
 
Orders the Hellenic Republic to pay the costs.
