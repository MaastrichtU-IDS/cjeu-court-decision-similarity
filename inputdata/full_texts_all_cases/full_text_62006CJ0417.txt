
Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of November – Italy v Commission
(Case C‑417/P)
Appeal – Admissibility – Structural Funds – Financing of Community initiatives – Amendment of indicative allocations – Enforcement of the final judgment
1.                     Actions for annulment – Judgment annulling a measure – Effects (Art. EC) (see paras 50-53, 59-60, 65-66)
2.                     Appeals – Grounds – Insufficient or contradictory statement of reasons (Art. EC; Statute of the Court of Justice, Art.
            58, first para.; Rules of Procedure of the Court, Art. 112(1)(c), first para.) (see paras 75-76)
Re: 
Appeal against the judgment of the Court of First Instance (Fourth Chamber) of July in Case T-225/Italy 
v 
Commission
 dismissing the action for annulment of Commission Decision C(2003) final of November establishing indicative
                  allocations between the Member States of the commitment appropriations under Community initiatives for the period 1994-and of all related prior measures.
               
Operative part 
 
Appeal dismissed;
 
The Italian Republic is ordered to pay its own costs and those of the Commission of the European Communities.
