
Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of July – Commission v Italy
(Case C‑371/05)
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Directive 92/50/EEC – Articles and 15(2) – Public service contracts – Award of IT services for the commune of Mantova (Italy) – Direct award without prior publication of a notice to tender
Approximation of laws – Procedures for the award of public service contracts – Directive 92/– Scope – Contracting authority holding shares in a company which is legally separate from it with one or more bodies constituted under
         private law (Council Directive 92/50) (see paras 22, 24, 26, 29-33)
      
Re:
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Infringement of Articles and 15(2) of Council Directive 92/50/EEC of
                  June relating to the coordination of procedures for the award of public service contracts (OJ L 209, p. 1) –
                  Award of IT services for the Commune di Mantova – Direct award without prior publication of a contract notice.
               
Operative part: 
The Court: 
1.
	Dismisses the action;
2.
	Orders the Commission of the European Communities to pay the costs.
