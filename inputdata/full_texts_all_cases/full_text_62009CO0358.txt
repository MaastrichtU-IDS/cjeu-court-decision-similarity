
Order of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of July – DSV Road v Commission
(Case C-358/P)
Appeal – Customs Code – Import of diskettes originating in Thailand – Post-clearance recovery of import duties – Application for remission of import duties
1.                     Appeals – Grounds – Error of law relied on not identified – Inadmissibility (Art. EC; Statute of the Court of Justice,
            Art. 58, first para.; Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice, Art. 112(1)(c)) (see paras 45-46)
2.                     Own resources of the European Communities – Post-clearance recovery of import or export duties – Conditions for non-entry
            of import duties in the accounts set out in Article 220(2)(b) of Regulation No 2913/– Burden of proof (Council Regulation
            No 2913/92, Art. 220(2)(b)) (see paras 47-52, 58-59)
3.                     Appeals – Grounds – Incorrect assessment of the evidence duly produced – Inadmissible except in the case of clear distortion
            of the sense ‑ Duty of the General Court to state the reasons for its assessment of the evidence – Extent (Art. EC; Statute
            of the Court of Justice, Art. 58, first para.) (see paras 68-69)
4.                     Own resources of the European Communities – Repayment or remission of import duties – Fairness clause introduced by Article
            of the Customs Code – Special situation – Meaning – Incorrect certificates issued by the customs authorities on the basis
            of the exporter’s untrue declarations, but those authorities not obliged to check the validity of those declarations – Excluded
            (Council Regulation No 2913/92, Art. 239; Commission Regulation No 2454/93, Art. 904(c)) (see paras 79-83)
Re: 
Appeal against the judgment of July of the Court of First Instance (Fourth Chamber) in Case T–219/DSV Road
 v 
Commission 
dismissing an application for annulment of the Commission’s Decision of April informing the Belgian authorities that
                  they might proceed with post-clearance recovery of import duties on diskettes originating in Thailand and that there were
                  no grounds for granting remission of those duties (File reference: REC 05/02).
               
Operative part: 
The Court:
1.
	Dismisses the appeal;
2.
	Orders DSV Road NV to pay the costs.
