On January 2019, the Court of Justice of the European Union (First Chamber) delivered its judgment in M.A. and Others (C‑661/17, EU:C:2019:53).
That judgment contains, in the version in the language of the case, an error which it is appropriate for the Court to rectify of its own motion in accordance with Article 103(1) of the Rules of Procedure of the Court.
 
On those grounds, the Court (First Chamber) hereby orders:
 
1.
In the introductory part of the judgment of January 2019, M.A. and Others (C‑661/17, EU:C:2019:53), the reference to the observations of Ireland shall be rectified as follows:
‘Ireland, by M. Browne, G. Hodge and A. Joyce, acting as Agents, and by S.-J. Hillery, Barrister-at-Law, and D. Conlan Smyth, Senior Counsel,’
 
2.
The original of this order shall be annexed to the original of the rectified judgment. A note of this order shall be made in the margin of the original of the rectified judgment.
 
Luxembourg, March 2019.
