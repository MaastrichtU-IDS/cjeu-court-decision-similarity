
Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of July – Poland v Parliament and Council
(Case C‑460/05)
Directive 2005/36/EC – Recognition of professional qualifications – Nurses responsible for general care – Midwives – Specific provisions applicable to Polish qualifications – Validity – Duty to give reasons – Introduced by Act of Accession
Acts of the Community institutions – Statement of reasons – Obligation – Scope (Art. EC; European Parliament and Council Directive 2005/36) (see paras 18-21)
Re: 
Annulment of Articles 33(2) and 43(3) of Directive 2005/36/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of September
                  on the recognition of professional qualifications (OJ L 255, p. 22) – Special rules for the recognition of the acquired
                  rights of nurses responsible for general care and midwives who have Polish qualifications.
               
Operative part
The Court:
 
Dismisses the action.
 
Orders the Republic of Poland to pay the costs.
 
Orders the Commission of the European Communities to bear its own costs.
