
Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of July —Rügen Fisch v OHIM(Case C-582/P)
Appeal — Regulation (EC) No 40/— Article 7(1) and (2) — Community trade mark — Word mark SCOMBER MIX — Absolute ground for invalidity — Descriptive character
Appeals — Grounds — Incorrect assessment of the facts and evidence — Inadmissibility — Review by the Court of the assessment of the facts and evidence — Possible only where the clear sense of the evidence has been distorted (Art. 256(1) TFEU; Statute of the Court of Justice,
         Art. 58, first para.) (para. 46)
      
Re: 
Appeal brought against the judgment of the General Court (Third Chamber) of September in Case T-201/Rügen Fisch 
v
 OHIM
, by which the General Court dismissed the appellant’s action against the decision of the Fourth Board of Appeal of OHIM of
                  March (Case R 230/2007-4), relating to invalidity proceedings between Rügen Fisch AG and Schwaaner Fischwaren GmbH —
                  Infringment of Articles 7(1)(c) and 51(1)(a) of Council Regulation (EC) No 207/of February on the Community trade
                  mark (OJ L 78, p. 1) — Descriptive character of the word sign SCOMBER MIX.
               
Operative part
1.
	The appeal is dismissed.
2.
	Rügen Fisch AG is ordered to pay the costs.
