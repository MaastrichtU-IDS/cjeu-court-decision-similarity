
Order of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of December – SC DRA SPEED v Direcţia Generală a Finanţelor Publice Bacău and Administraţia
            Finanţelor Publice Bacău
(Case C‑439/10)
Reference for a preliminary ruling – Failure to provide a factual description – Inadmissibility
Questions referred for a preliminary ruling – Admissibility – Questions asked without enough detail given of the facts – Manifest inadmissibility (Art. TFEU; Statute of the Court of Justice, Art. 23; Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice,
         Arts 92(1) and 103(1)) (see paras 9-12, operative part)
      
Re: 
Reference for a preliminary ruling – Curtea de Apel Secţia Bacău Secţia Comercială, Contencios Administrativ şi Fiscal – Registration
                  of second-hand vehicles previously registered in other Member States – Environmental tax on vehicles on their first registration
                  in a Member State – Compatibility of the national rules with Article TFEU – Discrimination in relation to second-hand
                  vehicles already registered in the territory of that Member State and not subject to that tax on a subsequent sale or new
                  registration.
               
Operative part
 
	The reference for a preliminary ruling made by the Curtea de Apel Bacău by decision of September is manifestly inadmissible.
