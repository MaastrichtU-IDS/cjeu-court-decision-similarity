
Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of May – Commission v Finland
(Case C-343/05)
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Directive 2001/37/EC – Manufacture, presentation and sale of tobacco products – Prohibition on placing on the tobacco market for oral use – Failure to transpose within the prescribed period
Member States – Obligations – Implemention of directives – Failure to fulfil obligations not contested (Art. EC) (see paras 8-9)
Re:
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Failure to transpose Article 8a of Council Directive 89/622/EEC of November
                  on the approximation of the laws, regulations and administrative provisions of the Member States concerning the labelling
                  of tobacco products (OJ L 359, p. 1) as inserted by Council Directive 92/41/EEC of May amending that directive
                  (OJ L 158, p. 30), and Article of Directive 2001/37/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of June on the approximation of the laws, regulations and administrative provisions of the Member States concerning the manufacture,
                  presentation and sale of tobacco products – Commission statement (OJ L 194, p. 26) – Prohibition on placing on the tobacco
                  market for oral use in relation to the Åland Islands and vessels registered in Finland.
               
Operative part
The Court:
1.
Declares that, by failing to ensure transposition by the Åland Islands of Article 8a of Directive 89/622/EEC of November
                     on the approximation of the laws, regulations and administrative provisions of the Member States concerning the labelling
                     of tobacco products, as amended by Council Directive 92/41/EEC of May and replicated in Article of Directive 2001/37/EC
                     of the European Parliament and of the Council of June on the approximation of the laws, regulations and administrative
                     provisions of the Member States concerning the manufacture, presentation and sale of tobacco products, and observance on vessels
                     registered in Finland of the prohibition on placing on the market of snuff laid down by that provision, the Republic of Finland
                     has failed to fulfil its obligations under the EC Treaty and Directive 2001/37;
                  
2.
Orders the Republic of Finland to pay the costs.
