IN CASE 276/COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES ,   APPLICANT ,   V  HELLENIC REPUBLIC ,   DEFENDANT .  
Grounds
BY LETTER OF JULY , WHICH WAS RECEIVED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON JULY , THE APPLICANT INFORMED THE COURT THAT IT WAS WITHDRAWING ITS ACTION AND REQUESTED THAT THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC BE ORDERED TO PAY THE COSTS .  BY TELECOPIER MESSAGE OF AUGUST , WHICH WAS RECEIVED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON THE SAME DAY , THE GOVERNMENT OF THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC INFORMED THE COURT THAT IT CONSENTS TO THE WITHDRAWAL OF THE ACTION BY THE COMMISSION AND THAT IT LEAVES THE QUESTION OF COSTS TO THE COURT .
