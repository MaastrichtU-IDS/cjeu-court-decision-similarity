
Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of May – Kronberger v Parliament
(Case C‑349/P)
Appeal – European Parliament – Elections – Challenge – Appeal in part manifestly inadmissible and in part manifestly unfounded
Applications for interim measures – Conditions governing admissibility (Arts EC and EC; Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice, Art. 83(1)) (see
         paras 3-4)
      
Re: 
APPEAL against the order of the Court of First Instance (Seventh Chamber) of May in Case T-18/Kronberger
 v 
Parliament
, by which that Court dismissed as inadmissible the action for annulment of the decision of the European Parliament of April rejecting as unfounded the applicant’s challenge relating to the election of Mr Andreas Mölzer as a Member of the
                  European Parliament, submitted in accordance with the provisions of Article of the Act of September concerning
                  the election of the representatives of the Parliament by direct universal suffrage.
               
Operative part
The Court:
1.
	Dismisses the appeal;
2.
	Orders Mr Kronberger to pay the costs.
