
Order of the President of the Court of April – Pellegrini v Commission
(Case C‑114/P(R)-INT)
Procedure – Application for interpretation – Conditions for the admissibility thereof – Inadmissibility
Procedure – Interpretation of a judgment – Conditions for the admissibility thereof (Statute of the Court of Justice, Art. 43) (see para. 6)
Re: 
Application for interpretation of the order of the President of the Court of First Instance of January 2008, in Case T-375/R 
               
Rosario Maria Pellegrini 
v 
Commission
, rejecting the application for interim measures requesting that the Commission be ordered to pay compensation, on a provisional
                  basis, for the financial damage allegedly suffered by the applicant because of the Commission’s failure to ensure full application
                  and correct interpretation of the provisions of Community law governing the activities of financial intermediaries.
               
Operative part 
 
	The Court:
1.
	Dismisses the application for interpretation;
2.
	Orders Mr Pellegrini to bear his own costs.
