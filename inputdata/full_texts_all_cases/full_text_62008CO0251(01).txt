
Order of the President of the Court of March – Ayyanarsamy v Commission and Germany
(Case C‑251/P-R)
Application for interim measures – Inadmissibility of the main action
Applications for interim measures – Conditions of admissibility – Main action dismissed as inadmissible (Art. EC and EC; Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice, Art. 83(1) (see
         paras 3-4)
      
Re: 
Application for interim measures – Application for interim measures seeking to obtain, first, suspension of the operation
                  of the letter of the Commission of September informing the appellant that it could not intervene in his favour before
                  the German courts and, secondly, payment of provisional compensation.
               
Operative part:
1.
	There is no longer any need to adjudicate on the application for interim measures.
2.
	Mr Ayyanarsamy is ordered to bear his own costs.
