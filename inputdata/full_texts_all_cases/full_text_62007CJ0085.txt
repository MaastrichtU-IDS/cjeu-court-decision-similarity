
Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of December – Commission v Italy
(Case C‑85/07)
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Directive 2000/60/EC – Articles 5(1) and 15(2) – Community action in the field of water policy – River basin district – Summary report and analyses – Communication thereof – None
Actions for failure to fulfil obligations – Examination of merits by the Court – Situation to be taken into consideration – Situation on expiry of the period laid down in the reasoned opinion (Art. EC) (see para. 13)
Re:
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Infringement of Articles 5(1) and 15(2) of Directive 2000/60/EC of the European
                  Parliament and of the Council of October establishing a framework for Community action in the field of water policy
                  (OJ L 327, p. 1) – Failure to submit summary reports on the analyses required under Article as regards certain river
                  basin districts – Failure to carry out the analyses and studies provided for in Article 5(1) of that directive.
               
Operative part
The Court: 
 
Declares that, in respect of the pilot river basin district of the River Serchio and a part of the river basin districts of
                     the Eastern Alps and the Northern, Central and Southern Apennines, by failing to submit a summary report on the analyses required
                     under Article 5(1) of Directive 2000/60/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of October establishing a
                     framework for Community action in the field of water policy, as provided for in Article 15(2) thereof, and by failing to carry
                     out the analyses and the study referred to in Article 5(1) thereof, the Italian Republic has failed to fulfil its obligations
                     under Articles 5(1) and 15(2) of that directive;
                  
 
Orders the Italian Republic to pay the costs.
