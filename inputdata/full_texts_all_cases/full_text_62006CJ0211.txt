
JUDGMENT OF THE COURT (Eighth Chamber)
January Case C-211/P
Herta Adam
v
Commission of the European Communities 
(Appeal – Officials – Remuneration – Expatriation allowance – Condition provided for by the second indent of Article 4(1)(a) of Annex VII to the Staff Regulations – Definition of ‘work done for another State’)
Appeal:      against the judgment of the Court of First Instance (First Chamber) in Case T‑342/Adam v Commission [2006] ECR-SC I-A-2-and II-A-2-107, dismissing an action seeking the annulment of the Commission decision of September
         refusing the applicant the benefit of the expatriation allowance provided for by Article 4(1) of Annex VII to the Staff
         Regulations of Officials of the European Communities.
      
Held: The appeal is dismissed. Ms Adam is ordered to pay the costs.
