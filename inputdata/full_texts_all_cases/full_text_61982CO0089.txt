ULRICH WOLKER , HEIDELBERG , APPLICANT ,   V  COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES ,   DEFENDANT ,   
Grounds
APPLICATION TO SUSPEND THE OPERATION OF A DECISION BY WHICH THE COMMISSION ORDERED THAT THE APPLICANT BE NOT ADMITTED TO THE TESTS FOR COMPETITION COM/A/- LEGAL ASPECTS .  BY A TELEX MESSAGE RECEIVED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON MARCH THE COMMISSION NOTIFIED THE COURT THAT IT HAD DECIDED AT ITS MEETING ON MARCH TO POSTPONE THE WRITTEN TESTS FOR THE COMPETITION AT ISSUE .  IN THESE CIRCUMSTANCES THE DEFENDANT TAKES THE VIEW THAT THE APPLICATION FOR THE ADOPTION OF INTERIM MEASURES HAS LOST ITS PURPOSE FOR THE TIME BEING AND ASKS THE COURT TO DECLARE THAT THERE ARE NO GROUNDS FOR COMING TO A DECISION ON THIS APPLICATION AT THE PRESENT TIME .  BY LETTER OF MARCH , RECEIVED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON MARCH , THE APPLICANT DECLARED THAT THE PROCEEDINGS ON THE INTERLOCUTORY MEASURE WERE AT AN END AND CLAIMED THAT THE COURT SHOULD ORDER THE DEFENDANT TO PAY THE COSTS ARISING FROM THE PROCEEDINGS ON THE APPLICATION FOR THE ADOPTION OF AN INTERIM MEASURE .      BY LETTER OF APRIL , REGISTERED AT THE COURT ON APRIL , THE DEFENDANT CONTENDED THAT THE COSTS SHOULD BE RESERVED . IT STATED THAT THE APPLICANT WAS IN AGREEMENT ON THIS POINT .  
Operative part
THE PRESIDENT OF THE SECOND CHAMBER OF THE COURT HEREBY ORDERS AS FOLLOWS :   . THERE ARE NO GROUNDS FOR COMING TO A DECISION ON THIS APPLICATION FOR THE ADOPTION OF AN INTERIM MEASURE .  2.THE COSTS ARE RESERVED .
