
Order of the President of the Court of December – Commission v Italy
(Case C-573/R)
Application for interim measures – Interim measures – Conservation of wild birds – Directive 79/409/EEC – Derogations from the system of protection
1.                     Application for interim measures – Interim measures – Conditions for granting – Prima facie case – Urgency – Serious and irreparable
            damage – Cumulative nature – Weighing up of all the interests involved (Art. TFEU) (see paras 11-12)
2.                     Environment – Conservation of wild birds – Directive 79/– Implementation by Member States – Derogations from the general
            system of protection – Conditions for granting (Council Directive 79/409, Art. 9(1) and (2)) (see paras 15-16, 23-24)
3.                     Application for interim measures – Interim measures – Conditions for granting – Urgency – Serious and irreparable damage –
            Burden of proof placed on the applicant (Art. TFEU) (see paras 17-18, 25-26)
Re: 
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Infringement of Arts 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 9, 10, 11, and of Council Directive
                  79/409/EEC of April on the conservation of wild birds (OJ L 103, p. 1) – Incorrect transposition – Derogations
                  – Requirements.
               
Operative part
The Court:
1.
	Orders the Italian Republic to suspend the application of Article 4(1) of the Law of the Regione di Lombardia No of July regulating the derogation system provided for by Article of Council Directive 79/409/EEC on the conservation of
                     wild birds, pursuant to Law No of October (Disciplina del regime di deroga previsto dall’articolo della direttiva
                     79/409/CEE del Consiglio, del aprile 1979, concernente la conservazione degli uccelli selvatici, in attuazione della legge
                     ottobre 2002, n. 221), as amended for the hunting season 2009/by Law No of the Regione di Lombardia of September
                     2009;
                  
2.
	Reserves the costs.
