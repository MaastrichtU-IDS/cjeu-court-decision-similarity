Grounds
ON JANUARY THE COMMISSION TOOK A DECISION UNDER ARTICLE OF THE EEC TREATY ( IV/28.AEG-TELEFUNKEN ) DISAPPROVING THE SYSTEM OF     SELECTIVE DISTRIBUTION ESTABLISHED WITHIN THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITY FOR PRODUCTS OF THE TELEFUNKEN BRAND .   ACCORDING TO ARTICLE OF THE DECISION AEG IS REQUIRED TO BRING THE INFRACTION IN QUESTION TO AN END FORTHWITH . ARTICLE IMPOSES ON AEG A FINE AMOUNTING TO ( ONE MILLION ) EUROPEAN CURRENCY UNITS , OR DM ( TWO MILLION FOUR HUNDRED AND FORTY-FIVE THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED AND EIGHTY GERMAN MARKS ), WHICH MUST BE PAID WITHIN THREE MONTHS OF THE NOTIFICATION OF THE DECISION . ARTICLE PROVIDES THAT THE DECISION IS TO BE ENFORCEABLE IN PURSUANCE OF ARTICLE OF THE EEC TREATY .   IN THESE CIRCUMSTANCES THE FINE MUST BE PAID AT THE LATEST BY APRIL , FROM WHICH DATE ENFORCEMENT WILL BECOME POSSIBLE .   BY APPLICATION LODGED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON MARCH THE APPLICANT HAS BROUGHT AN ACTION FOR A DECLARATION THAT THE COMMISSION ' S DECISION IS VOID . BY A SEPARATE DOCUMENT LODGED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON THE SAME DAY THE APPLICANT APPLIED IN PURSUANCE OF THE FOURTH PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE OF THE EEC TREATY AND ARTICLE ET SEQ . OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE OF THE COURT OF JUSTICE FOR A SUSPENSION OF THE OPERATION OF THE CONTESTED DECISION WITHOUT THE APPLICANT ' S BEING REQUIRED TO LODGE SECURITY TO OBTAIN A SUSPENSION OF THE OBLIGATION TO PAY THE FINE .   ACCORDING TO ARTICLE ( ) OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE THE PRESIDENT MAY GRANT THE APPLICATION FOR SUSPENSION EVEN BEFORE THE OBSERVATIONS OF THE OPPOSITE PARTY HAVE BEEN SUBMITTED . SUCH A DECISION MAY BE VARIED OR CANCELLED EVEN WITHOUT ANY APPLICATION ' S BEING MADE BY ANY PARTY .   IN THE INTERESTS OF THE PROPER ADMINISTRATION OF JUSTICE IT IS NECESSARY , AS A PRECAUTIONARY MEASURE , TO SUSPEND THE OPERATION OF ARTICLE OF THE DISPUTED MEASURE UNTIL THE COURT ARRIVES AT ITS DECISION ON THE WHOLE OF THE APPLICATION FOR THE ADOPTION OF INTERIM MEASURES .  
Operative part
ON THOSE GROUNDS , THE PRESIDENT ,   BY WAY OF INTERIM DECISION ,   HEREBY ORDERS AS FOLLOWS :   . THE OPERATION OF ARTICLE OF THE COMMISSION DECISION OF JANUARY RELATING TO A PROCEEDING UNDER ARTICLE OF THE EEC TREATY ( IV/28.AEG-TELEFUNKEN ) SHALL , AS A PRECAUTIONARY MEASURE , BE SUSPENDED UNTIL THE COURT ARRIVES AT ITS DECISION ON THE WHOLE OF THE APPLICATION FOR THE ADOPTION OF INTERIM MEASURES .  2.THE PROCEEDINGS WITH REGARD TO THE APPLICATION FOR THE ADOPTION OF INTERIM MEASURES SHALL BE PURSUED IMMEDIATELY . THE COMMISSION SHALL LODGE ITS WRITTEN OBSERVATIONS BEFORE APRIL .   3.THE COSTS ARE RESERVED .
