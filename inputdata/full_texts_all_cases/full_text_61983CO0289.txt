Grounds
BY AN APPLICATION LODGED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON DECEMBER , GAARM AND OTHER APPLICANTS BROUGHT AN ACTION FOR DAMAGES UNDER ARTICLE AND THE SECOND PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE OF THE EEC TREATY . THE APPLICATION WAS NOT ACCOMPANIED BY THE DOCUMENTS MENTIONED IN ARTICLE ( ) OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE , NAMELY ( I ) THE INSTRUMENTS CONSTITUTING AND REGULATING THE COMPANIES IN QUESTION AND ( II ) PROOF THAT THE AUTHORITY GRANTED TO THE APPLICANTS '  LAWYER WAS PROPERLY CONFERRED ON HIM BY SOMEONE AUTHORIZED FOR THE PURPOSE .  BY A LETTER DATED JANUARY THE REGISTRAR OF THE COURT REQUESTED THE APPLICANTS , PURSUANT TO ARTICLE ( ) OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE , TO PRODUCE THOSE DOCUMENTS BY JANUARY . THE RELEVANT DOCUMENTS WERE     PRODUCED TO THE COURT BY OF THE APPLICANTS ON JANUARY ; THREE OTHER APPLICANTS PRODUCED THE DOCUMENTS ON JUNE . NO DOCUMENT HAD BEEN LODGED BY THAT DATE BY THE FOLLOWING COMPANIES :   . UNION DES PRODUCTEURS VENDEURS DE FRUITS ET LEGUMES  15.GROUPEMENT DES PRODUCTEURS DE POMMES DE TERRE DE PRIMEUR DU BASSIN DE L ' ADOUR  19.SOREP - SYNDICAT POUR L ' ORGANISATION ECONOMIQUE DES PRODUCTIONS  22.GROUPEMENT DE PRODUCTEURS DU SUD-EST DE FRUITS ET LEGUMES POUR LA CONSERVE ET LE MARCHE DU FRAIS  24.SYNDICAT POUR L ' ORGANISATION ECONOMIQUE DES PRODUCTEURS DE FRUITS ET LEGUMES DU ROUSSILLON , AND  25.SYNDICAT DES PRODUCTEURS DE LEGUMES ET FRUITS DE LA BASSE-VALLEE DU GARDON .  BY LETTER DATED JUNE THE REGISTRAR OF THE COURT GRANTED THE ABOVEMENTIONED APPLICANTS A PERIOD EXPIRING ON JUNE IN WHICH TO PRODUCE THE RELEVANT DOCUMENTS AND STATED THAT AFTER THAT DATE ' ' THEIR ACTION MAY BE DEEMED INADMISSIBLE UNDER ARTICLE ( ) OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE ' ' .  IN A LETTER DATED JUNE THE LAWYER REPRESENTING THE APPLICANTS ACKNOWLEDGED RECEIPT OF THE REGISTRAR ' S LETTER OF JUNE BUT MADE NO MENTION OF THE APPLICANTS '  INTENTION TO PRODUCE THE RELEVANT DOCUMENTS OR OF ANY DIFFICULTIES PREVENTING THEM FROM COMPLYING WITH THE TIME-LIMIT IMPOSED .  ARTICLE ( ) OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE PROVIDES THAT , IF AN APPLICATION DOES NOT COMPLY WITH THE REQUIREMENTS SET OUT IN PARAGRAPHS TO OF THAT ARTICLE AND IF THE APPLICANT FAILS TO PUT THE APPLICATION IN ORDER OR TO PRODUCE THE REQUISITE DOCUMENTS WITHIN A REASONABLE PERIOD PRESCRIBED BY THE REGISTRAR , THE COURT MAY REJECT THE APPLICATION ON THE GROUND OF WANT OF FORM .  IN THE PRESENT CASE , IT MUST BE NOTED THAT THE PERIOD FOR PUTTING THE APPLICATION IN ORDER , WHICH ORIGINALLY EXPIRED ON JANUARY , WAS SUBSEQUENTLY EXTENDED UNTIL JUNE . THE APPLICANTS CONCERNED WERE THEREFORE GIVEN ABUNDANT TIME TO PRODUCE THE DOCUMENTS REQUESTED .  IN THOSE CIRCUMSTANCES , THE COURT IS JUSTIFIED IN HOLDING THAT THE ACTION BROUGHT ON DECEMBER IS INADMISSIBLE AS REGARDS THE APPLICANTS WHICH HAVE NOT PRODUCED THE DOCUMENTS MENTIONED IN ARTICLE ( ).
