
Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of March – Organismos Sillogikis Diacheirisis Dimiourgon Theatrikon kai Optikoakoustikon
            Ergon v Divani Akropolis Anonimi Xenodocheiaki kai Touristiki Etaireai
(Case C‑136/09)
Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure – Copyright and related rights in the information society – Directive 2001/29/EC – Article – Concept of ‘communication to the public’ – Works communicated by means of television sets installed in hotel rooms
Approximation of laws – Copyright and related rights – Directive 2001/(European Parliament and Council Directive 2001/29, Art. 3(1)) (see para. 43, operative part)
Re: 
Reference for a preliminary ruling – Arios Pagos – Interpretation of Article 3(1) of Directive 2001/29/EC of the European
                  Parliament and of the Council of May on the harmonisation of certain aspects of copyright and related rights in the
                  information society (OJ L 167, p. 10) – Concept of ‘communication to the public’ – Works transmitted by means of television
                  sets installed in hotel rooms and connected to the central antenna of the hotel with no other action by the hotel to ensure
                  reception of a signal by hotel customers.
               
Operative part: 
The hotelier, by installing televisions in his hotel rooms and by connecting them to the central antenna of the hotel, thereby,
         and without more, carries out an act of communication to the public within the meaning of Article 3(1) of Directive 2001/29/EC
         of the European Parliament and of the Council of May on the harmonisation of certain aspects of copyright and related
         rights in the information society.
