
Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of January — Commission v Slovenia
(Case C‑185/11)
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations — Direct insurance other than life assurance — Directives 73/239/EEC and 92/49/EEC — Incorrect and incomplete transposition
Actions for failure to fulfil obligations — Examination of the merits by the Court — Situation to be taken into consideration — Situation on expiry of the period laid down in the reasoned opinion (Art. TFEU) (see para 21)
Re:
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations — Infringement of Articles and of the Treaty on the functioning of the
                  European Union — Infringement of Article 8(3) of First Council Directive 73/239/EEC of July on the coordination of
                  laws, regulations and administrative provisions relating to the taking-up and pursuit of the business of direct insurance
                  other than life assurance (OJ L 228, p. 3) and of Articles and of Council Directive 92/49/EEC of June on
                  the coordination of laws, regulations and administrative provisions relating to direct insurance other than life assurance
                  and amending Directives 73/239/EEC and 88/357/EEC (third non-life insurance Directive; OJ L 228, p. 1)
               
Operative part
The Court: 
1.
	Declares that, by transposing into national law incorrectly and incompletely First Council Directive 73/239/EEC of July
                     on the coordination of laws, regulations and administrative provisions relating to the taking-up and pursuit of the business
                     of direct insurance other than life assurance, as amended by Directive 2005/68/EC of the European Parliament and the Council
                     of November 2005, and Council Directive 92/49/EEC of June on the coordination of laws, regulations and administrative
                     provisions relating to direct insurance other than life assurance and amending directives 73/239/EEC and 88/357/EEC (third
                     non‑life insurance Directive), as amended by Directive 2005/68, the Republic of Slovenia has failed to fulfil its obligations
                     under Article 8(3) of Directive 73/and Articles and of Directive 92/49;
                  
2.
	Dismisses the remainder of the action;
3.
	Orders the European Commission and the Republic of Slovenia to bear their own costs.
