
Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of July – Ruipérez Aguirre and ATC Petition v Commission
(Case C-111/P)
Appeal – Action for annulment – Commission’s refusal to bring infringement proceedings – Inadmissibility – Appeal manifestly unfounded
Actions for annulment – Actionable measures – Commission’s refusal to bring infringement proceedings – Excluded (Arts TFEU and TFEU) (see paras 11-16)
Re: 
Appeal against the judgment of the General Court (Fourth Chamber) of January in Case T-487/Ruipérez Aguirre and ATC Petition
 v 
Commission, 
by which that court dismissed an action for the annulment of the decisions of the Commission of July and August 2010,
                  refusing to initiate infringement proceedings against the Kingdom of Spain under Article TFEU.
               
Operative part
 
	The Court:
1.
	Dismisses the appeal; 
2.
	Orders Mr Ruipérez Aguirre and ATC Petition to pay their own costs.
