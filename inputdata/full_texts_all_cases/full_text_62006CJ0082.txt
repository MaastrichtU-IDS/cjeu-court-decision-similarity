
Judgment of the Court (Eight Chamber) of June – Commission v Italy
(Case C‑82/06)
Failure of a Member State to fulfil its obligations – Environment – Waste – Hazardous waste – Directives 75/442/EEC and 91/689/EEC – Obligation to draw up and communicate waste management plans
1.                     Actions for failure to fulfil obligations – Subject-matter of the dispute – Determination during the procedure prior to the
            action (Art. EC) (see para. 14)
2.                     Acts of the Community institutions – Directives – Implementation by Member States (Art. EC; Council Directive 75/442,
            as amended by Directives 91/156, and 91/689) (see paras 19, 34-35, 37-39)
3.                     Actions for failure to fulfil obligations – Examination of merits by the Court – Situation to be taken into consideration
            – Situation on expiry of the period laid down in the reasoned opinion (Art. EC) (see para. 25)
4.                     Member States – Obligations – Implementation of directives – Failure to fulfil obligations – National system pleaded as justification
            – Not permissible (Art. EC) (see para. 26)
Re:
Failure of a Member State to fulfil its obligations – Infringement of Article of Council Directive 75/442/EEC of July
                  on waste (OJ L 194, p. 47), as amended by Council Directive 91/156/EEC of March (OJ L 78, p. 32), and
                  Article of Council Directive 91/689/EEC of December on hazardous waste (OJ L 377, p. 20) – Obligation to communicate
                  waste management plans.
               
Operative part 
The Court:
 
Declares that, by failing to draw up:
–       a waste management plan in accordance with Article 7(1) of Council Directive 75/442/EEC of July on waste, as amended
         by Council Directive 91/156/EEC of March 1991, for the Province of Rimini;
      
–       waste management plans including suitable sites or installations for the disposal of waste in accordance with the fourth indent
         of Article 7(1) of Directive 75/442, as amended by Directive 91/156, for the Region of Lazio;
      
–       waste management plans in accordance with Article of Council Directive 91/689/EEC of December on hazardous waste
         for the Regions of Friuli Venezia-Giulia and Puglia, the Autonomous Province of Bolzano Alto-Adige and the Province of Rimini,
      
the Italian Republic has failed to fulfil its obligations under those directives;
 
Orders the Italian Republic to pay the costs.
