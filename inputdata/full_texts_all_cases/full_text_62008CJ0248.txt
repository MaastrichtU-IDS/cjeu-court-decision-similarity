
Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of December – Commission v Greece
(Case C-248/08)
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Regulation (EC) No 1774/– Articles 4(2)(a) and (c), 5(2)(c), 6(2)(b), to 15, 17, and – Animal by-products – Waste – Burial without prior treatment – Lack of official controls – Plants for the safe management of animal by-products – Operation – Lack of authorisation – Incineration of specified risk materials – No adequate process
1.                     Actions for failure to fulfil obligations – Action in respect of an administrative practice contrary to Community law – Whether
            permissible – Conditions (Arts EC, EC and EC) (see paras 21-22, 26-28, 58)
2.                     Protection of public health – Health rules concerning animal by-products not intended for human consumption – Member States
            obliged to provide the suitable infrastructure and to adopt all measures necessary in order to comply with those rules – Obligation
            of result (European Parliament and Council Regulation No 1774/2002, Arts 4(2)(a) and (c), 5(2)(c), 6(2)(b), to 15, 17,
            and 26) (see paras 34-37, 48-49, 56-59, 66, 68)
Re: 
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Breach of Articles 4(2), 5(2), to 15, 17, and of Regulation (EC)
                  No 1774/of the European Parliament and of the Council of October laying down health rules concerning animal by-products
                  not intended for human consumption (OJ L 273, p. 1) – Burial of animal by-products without prior treatment – Lack of
                  official controls.
               
Operative part:
The Court: 
1.
	Declares that, by not correctly applying and imposing Regulation (EC) No 1774/of the European Parliament and of the Council
                     of October laying down health rules concerning animal by-products not intended for human consumption, in so far as
                     it regards burial in landfills without prior treatment, lack of official controls, authorisation of plants for the management
                     of animal by-products and incineration of specified risk materials, the Hellenic Republic has failed to fulfil its obligations
                     under Articles 4(2)(a) and (c), 5(2)(c), 6(2)(b), to 15, 17, and of Regulation No 1774/2002;
                  
2.
	Orders the Hellenic Republic to pay the costs.
