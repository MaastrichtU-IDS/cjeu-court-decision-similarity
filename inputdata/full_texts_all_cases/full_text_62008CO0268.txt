
ORDER OF THE COURT (Eighth Chamber) 
March Case C-268/P
Christos Michail
v
Commission of the European Communities 
(Appeal – Civil service – Articles 12a and of the Staff Regulations – Psychological harassment – Duty to provide assistance – Distortion of facts – Errors in the legal assessment of the facts)
Appeal: against the judgment of the Court of First Instance (First Chamber) of April in Case T‑486/Michail v Commission by which that court dismissed the appellant’s action for annulment of the Commission’s implied rejection of March of the appellant’s application for assistance under Article of the Staff Regulations. Infringement of Article of the
         Staff Regulations. Psychological harassment. Distortion of the facts. Errors in the legal classification of the facts 
      
Held:  The appeal is dismissed. Mr Michail is ordered to pay the costs.
      
Summary
Appeals – Grounds – Mere repetition of the pleas and arguments put forward before the Court of First Instance – Error of law
            relied on not identified – Inadmissible 
(Art. EC; Statute of the Court of Justice, Art. 58, first para; Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice, Art. 112(1),
            first para., (c))
