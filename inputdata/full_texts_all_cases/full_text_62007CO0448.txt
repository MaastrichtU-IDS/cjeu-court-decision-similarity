
Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of June – Ayuntamiento de Madrid and Madrid Calle v Commission
(Case C‑448/P)
Appeal – Provision of data relating to the excessive deficit procedure – Regulation (EC) No 3605/– European System of Accounts (ESA 1995) – Regulation (EC) No 2223/– Classification of the body ‘Madrid Calle 30’ in the ‘general government’ sector – Eurostat press release – Actionable act
Actions for annulment – Actionable measures – Measures producing binding legal effects – Commission press release publishing data relating to government debt and deficit – Implied decision approving the classification of a body in the general government sector of the European System of Accounts
         – None (Art. EC; Council Regulation No 3605/93, Arts 8a(1), 8c, 8g, 8h and 8i(1) (see paras 45-53)
      
Re:
Appeal against order of the Court of First Instance (Fourth Chamber) of July in Case T-177/Ayuntamiento de Madrid and Madrid Calle 30, SA
 v 
Commission
 dismissing as inadmissible an action for the annulment of the Eurostat news release No 48/of April to the extent
                  that it contains a decision of the Commission (Eurostat) on the classification of Madrid Calle in the ‘general government’
                  sector within the European System of Accounts (ESA 95).
               
Operative part
The Court: 
1.
	Dismisses the appeal;
2.
	Orders Ayuntamiento de Madrid and Madrid Calle 30, SA to pay the costs.
