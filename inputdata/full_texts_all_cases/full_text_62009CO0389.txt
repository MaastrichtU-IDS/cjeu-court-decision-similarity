
Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of January – Saenz Morales v Consejería para la Igualdad y Bienestar Social
(Case C‑389/09)
Preliminary rulings – Inadmissibility
Preliminary rulings – Admissibility – Questions referred without sufficient information on the factual and legislative context – Inadmissibility (Art. TFEU; Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice, Art. 92(1) and 103(1) (see paras 9-20)
Re:
Interpretation of Council Directive 1999/70/EC of June concerning the framework agreement on fixed-term work concluded
                  by ETUC, UNICE and CEEP (OJ L 175, p. 43) – Scope – Civil service of the Regional Government of Andalusia – Principle
                  of non‑discrimination.
               
Operative part
The reference for a preliminary ruling from the Juzgado de lo Contencioso-Administrativo No de Almería, by decision of September 2009, is manifestly inadmissible.
