Summary
As the Court has consistently held, a Member State may not plead provisions, practices or circumstances in its internal legal system in order to justify a failure to comply with obligations and time-limits laid down by Community directives .

( ) Declares that, by failing to forward to the Commission within the prescribed periods the reports needed, in relation to the Region of Brussels-Capital, in order to comply with Article of Council Directive 75/439/EEC of June on the disposal of waste oils, as amended by Council Directive 87/101/EEC of December 1986, Article of Council Directive 75/442/EEC of July on waste, Article of Council Directive 76/403/EEC of April on the disposal of polychlorinated biphenyls and polychlorinated terphenyls and Article of Council Directive 78/319/EEC of March on toxic and dangerous waste, the Kingdom of Belgium has failed to fulfil its obligations under the EEC Treaty;  ( ) Orders the Kingdom of Belgium to pay the costs .
