THE COURT (Eighth Chamber),
composed of C.G. Fernlund, President of the Eighth Chamber, acting as President of the Chamber, C. Toader (Rapporteur) and

Advocate General: N. Jääskinen,
Registrar: A. Calot Escobar,
after hearing the Advocate General,
makes the following
Order
On November 2013, the Court (Eighth Chamber) made the order in Case C‑617/Astrazeneca (not yet published in the ECR).
      
Paragraph of the grounds of the English version of the order contains an error, which must be rectified by the Court of
         its own motion in accordance with Article 154(1) of the Court’s Rules of Procedure.
      
On those grounds, the Court (Eighth Chamber) hereby orders:
1.      In the first sentence of paragraph of the English version of the order of November in Case C‑617/Astrazeneca, the words ‘recital 8’ must be replaced by the words ‘recital 9’.
2.      The original of the present order shall be annexed to the original of the rectified order. A note of the rectification order
            shall be made in the margin of the original rectified order.
[Signatures]
* Language of the case: English.
