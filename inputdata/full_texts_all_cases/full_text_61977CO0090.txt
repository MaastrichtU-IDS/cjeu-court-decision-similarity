IN CASE 90/, HELLMUT STIMMING KG , WITTEN ,   APPLICANT ,   V COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES ,   DEFENDANT ,   
Grounds
BY AN APPLICATION LODGED ON JULY , THE APPLICANT BROUGHT AN ACTION FOR DAMAGES ' AGAINST THE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES ' .   IN ITS REPLY IN THAT CASE , THE APPLICANT PURPORTS TO ' EXTEND '  ITS ACTION AND BRING IT ALSO AGAINST THE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES .   THE RULES OF PROCEDURE DO NOT ALLOW SUCH AN ALTERATION IN THE PERSON OF THE DEFENDANT .   ACCORDINGLY , THE REPLY IS TO BE ACCEPTED ONLY AS A PLEADING IN THE PROCEEDINGS BETWEEN THE APPLICANT AND THE COMMISSION , BUT CANNOT BE REGARDED AS COMMENCING AN ACTION AGAINST THE COUNCIL .  
Operative part
THE COURT HEREBY :   ORDERS THE REGISTRAR NOT TO SERVE ON THE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES THE REPLY LODGED ON NOVEMBER .
