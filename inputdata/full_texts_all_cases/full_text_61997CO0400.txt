Grounds
By orders of July 1997, received at the Court Registry on December 1997, the Tribunal Superior de Justicia del País Vasco (High Court of Justice of the Basque Country) referred three questions to the Court for preliminary rulings on the interpretation of Article of the EC Treaty (now, after amendment, Article EC) and of Article 92(1) of the EC Treaty (now, after amendment, Article 87(1) EC).By letters of February 2000, received at the Court Registry on February 2000, the Tribunal Superior de Justicia del País Vasco informed the Court that, as a result of the discontinuance of the applicant in the cases in the main proceedings, it was withdrawing its requests of July for preliminary rulings.In those circumstances, it must be ordered that the present cases be removed from the Register of the Court.The costs incurred by the Spanish Government and the Commission of the European Communities, which have submitted observations to the Court, are not recoverable. Since these proceedings are, for the parties to the main proceedings, a step in the actions pending before the national court, the decisions on costs are a matter for that court. 
Operative part
On those grounds,THE PRESIDENT OF THE COURThereby orders:Joined Cases C-400/97, C-401/and C-402/are removed from the Register of the Court.
