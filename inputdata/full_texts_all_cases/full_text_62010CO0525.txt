
Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of March – Noko Ngele v Commission
(Case C-525/P)
Appeals – Time-limit – Inadmissibility – Article of Rules of Procedure
Procedure – Time-limit for instituting proceedings – Appeals – Belatedness – Inadmissibility (Statute of the Court of Justice, Art. 56; Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice, Art. 81(2)) (see points
         4-8)
      
Re: 
Appeal against the order of the General Court (Third Chamber) of December in Case T-390/Noko Ngele
 v 
Commission
, by which that court dismissed the appellant’s application for compensation for the loss he suffered as a result of the allegedly
                  unlawful conduct of a member of the Commission and of certain of its staff – Failure to take account of an important item
                  of evidence in the Court file – Principle of fair legal process – Absolute nullity. 
               
Operative part
1.
	The appeal is dismissed. 
2.
	Noko Ngele is ordered to pay the costs.
