
Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of September – Correia de Matos v Parliament
(Case C‑502/P-INT)
Procedure – Application for interpretation – Lack of jurisdiction of the Court
Procedure – Interpretation of a judgment – Application for the interpretation of an order – Lack of jurisdiction of the Court (Statute of the Court of Justice, Art. 43; Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice,
         Art. 102) (see paras 6-8)
      
Re: 
Application for the interpretation of the order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of November in Case C-502/P 
Carlos Correia de Matos
 v 
European Parliament
. 
Operative part: 
1.
	The Court of Justice of the European Communities manifestly does not have jurisdiction to provide a reply to the application
                     for interpretation made by Mr Correia de Matos;
                  
2.
	Orders Mr Correia de Matos to bear his own costs.
