Subject of the case
APPLICATION FOR SUSPENSION OF THE OPERATION OF COMMISSION DECISION NO 86/OF JUNE ON THE CLEARANCE OF THE ACCOUNTS PRESENTED BY THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC IN RESPECT OF THE EUROPEAN AGRICULTURAL GUIDANCE AND GUARANTEE FUND , GUARANTEE SECTION , EXPENDITURE FOR , 
Grounds
ON JUNE , THE COMMISSION ADOPTED , UNDER ARTICLE ( ) OF COUNCIL REGULATION NO 729/OF APRIL ON THE FINANCING OF THE COMMON AGRICULTURAL POLICY ( OFFICIAL JOURNAL , ENGLISH SPECIAL EDITION ( I ), P . ), DECISION NO 86/ON THE CLEARANCE OF THE ACCOUNTS PRESENTED BY THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC IN RESPECT OF THE EUROPEAN AGRICULTURAL GUIDANCE AND GUARANTEE FUND , GUARANTEE SECTION , EXPENDITURE FOR . IN THAT DECISION , THE COMMISSION STATED THAT PART OF THE EXPENDITURE DECLARED , NAMELY DR , DID NOT FULFIL THE NECESSARY CONDITIONS AND THEREFORE COULD NOT BE CHARGED TO THE EAGGF , GUARANTEE SECTION .  BY AN APPLICATION LODGED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON AUGUST , THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC BROUGHT AN ACTION UNDER THE FIRST PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE OF THE EEC TREATY FOR A DECLARATION THAT THAT DECISION IS VOID .  BY AN APPLICATION LODGED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON AUGUST , THE APPLICANT , ACTING UNDER ARTICLE OF THE EEC TREATY , ARTICLE OF THE STATUTE OF THE COURT OF JUSTICE AND ARTICLE OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE , SOUGHT SUSPENSION OF THE OPERATION OF COMMISSION DECISION NO 86/UNTIL THE 30TH DAY FOLLOWING SERVICE OF THE COURT ' S JUDGMENT ON THE MAIN APPLICATION .  THE APPLICANT , IN ITS APPLICATION FOR INTERIM MEASURES , EMPHASIZES THAT THERE IS AN URGENT NEED TO OBTAIN SUSPENSION OF THE OPERATION OF THE DECISION NO 86/SINCE THE COMMISSION INTENDS TO PROCEED WITH THE IMMEDIATE IMPLEMENTATION THEREOF ON AUGUST BY DEDUCTING THE AMOUNTS WHICH THAT DECISION REFUSED TO CHARGE TO THE EAGGF , GUARANTEE SECTION , IN RESPECT OF EXPENDITURE FOR FROM THE AMOUNTS WHICH THE COMMISSION IS TO PAY ON THAT DATE FOR THE PURPOSE OF FINANCING AID IN THE FRAMEWORK OF THE COMMON AGRICULTURAL POLICY .  IN REPLY TO A QUESTION PUT TO IT ON AUGUST BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE COURT , THE COMMISSION STATED THAT FOR TECHNICAL REASONS IT HAD NOT BEEN POSSIBLE , IN THE CASE OF THREE MEMBER STATES , INCLUDING GREECE , TO GIVE EFFECT TO THE DECISIONS CLEARING THE ACCOUNTS FOR WHEN THE ADVANCE PAYMENT FOR AUGUST WAS FIXED AND THAT IT HAD ADOPTED A DECISION ON AUGUST CONCERNING THE ADVANCE PAYMENT FOR SEPTEMBER WHICH APPLIED TO THOSE THREE MEMBER STATES , INCLUDING GREECE , THE FINANCIAL CONSEQUENCES FLOWING FROM THE CLEARANCE OF THE ACCOUNTS .  IT THUS APPEARS THAT BY MEANS OF ITS DECISION FIXING THE ADVANCE PAYMENT FOR SEPTEMBER , ADOPTED ON AUGUST , THE COMMISSION INTENDS TO IMPLEMENT DECISION NO 86/AND WILL DEDUCT FROM THE ADVANCE PAYMENT FOR SEPTEMBER THE DR WHICH THE LATTER DECISION DID NOT ACCEPT AS CHARGEABLE TO THE EAGGF , GUARANTEE SECTION .  ACCORDING TO ARTICLE ( ) OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE , THE PRESIDENT MAY GRANT AN APPLICATION FOR INTERIM MEASURES EVEN BEFORE THE OBSERVATIONS OF THE OPPOSITE PARTY HAVE BEEN SUBMITTED . THAT DECISION MAY BE VARIED OR CANCELLED EVEN WITHOUT ANY APPLICATION BEING MADE BY ANY PARTY .  IN THIS CASE , IT IS DESIRABLE TO BE AWARE OF THE COMMISSION ' S WRITTEN OBSERVATIONS BEFORE RULING ON THE APPLICATION FOR INTERIM MEASURES . UNDER THOSE CIRCUMSTANCES AND HAVING REGARD TO THE PARTICULAR CHARACTERISTICS OF THE DISPUTE , IT IS NECESSARY , AS A PRECAUTIONARY MEASURE , IN THE INTERESTS OF THE PROPER ADMINISTRATION OF JUSTICE TO SUSPEND , UNTIL THE ORDER TERMINATING THE PRESENT APPLICATION FOR INTERIM MEASURES HAS BEEN MADE , OPERATION OF DECISION NO 86/, WHICH WOULD OTHERWISE BE IMPLEMENTED , ACCORDING TO THE INFORMATION SUPPLIED BY THE COMMISSION , BY MEANS OF THE DECISION CONCERNING THE ADVANCE PAYMENT FOR SEPTEMBER WHICH IT ADOPTED ON AUGUST .   
Operative part
ON THOSE GROUNDS , THE PRESIDENT ,   BY WAY OF INTERIM DECISION ,   HEREBY ORDERS AS FOLLOWS :    ( ) APPLICATION OF COMMISSION DECISION NO 86/OF JUNE ON THE CLEARANCE OF THE ACCOUNTS PRESENTED BY THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC IN RESPECT OF THE EUROPEAN AGRICULTURAL GUIDANCE AND GUARANTEE FUND , GUARANTEE SECTION , EXPENDITURE FOR BY MEANS OF THE DECISION PROVIDING FOR THE ADVANCE PAYMENT FOR SEPTEMBER ADOPTED BY THE COMMISSION ON AUGUST IS SUSPENDED , AS A PRECAUTIONARY MEASURE , UNTIL THE ORDER TERMINATING THE PRESENT PROCEEDINGS FOR INTERIM MEASURES HAS BEEN MADE .   ( 2)THE PROCEEDINGS FOR INTERIM MEASURES SHALL CONTINUE . THE COMMISSION MUST SUBMIT ITS WRITTEN OBSERVATIONS NOT LATER THAN AUGUST .    ( ) COSTS ARE RESERVED .
