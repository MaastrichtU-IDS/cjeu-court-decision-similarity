
Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of January – Implants (International) Ltd  v Commission
(Case C-279/OP)
Arbitration clause – Proceedings to have a judgment by default set aside – Reimbursement of an advance payment – Default interest

Application to set aside the judgment of the Court of February in Case C‑279/Commission
 v 
Implants
Operative part:
The Court:
1.
Dismisses the application by Implants (International) Ltd to set aside the judgment in Case C-279/Commission v Implants;
2.
Orders Implants (International) Ltd to pay the costs of the present proceedings.
