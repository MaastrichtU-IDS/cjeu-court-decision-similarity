
Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of July – SELEX Sistemi Integrati v Commission
(Case C‑481/P)
Appeal – Non-contractual liability of the Community – Commission Decision rejecting a complaint brought against Eurocontrol – Actual and certain damage
1.                     Non-contractual liability – Conditions – Causal link – Préjudice constitué par les frais afférents à la procédure précontentieuse
            (Art. 288, second para., EC; Rules of Procedure of the Court of First Instance, Art. 87) (see paras 20-23)
2.                     Non-contractual liability – Conditions – Unlawfulness – Injury/damage – Causal link (Art. 288, second para., EC; Rules of
            Procedure of the Court of First Instance, Art. 44(1)(e)) (see paras 36-43)
Re: 
Appeal against the order of the Court of First Instance (Second Chamber) of August in Case T-186/SELEX Sistemi Integrati SpA
 v 
Commission,
 by which the Court of First Instance dismissed as in part manifestly inadmissible and in part manifestly without foundation
                  in law the claim for damages for the loss purportedly suffered by the appellant as a result of the Commission’s decision of
                  February rejecting the appellant’s complaint relating to an alleged infringement by Eurocontrol of the EC Treaty provisions
                  on competition.
               
Operative part
The Court: 
1.
	Dismisses the appeal.
2.
	Orders SELEX Sistemi Integrati SpA to pay the costs.
