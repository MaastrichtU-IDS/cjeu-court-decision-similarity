
Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of February – Municipio de Barcelos v Estado Português
(Case C‑408/09)
Definition of ‘national jurisdiction’ – Manifest lack of jurisdiction
Preliminary rulings – Reference to the Court – National court or tribunal for the purposes of Article EC – Meaning – To be determined on the basis of criteria relating to that body’s constitution and function – Municipal body – No judicial function (Art. EC) (see paras 4-11)
Re: 
Interpretation of Directive 97/11/EC of March amending Directive 85/337/EEC on the assessment of the effects of certain
                  public and private projects on the environment (OJ L 73, p. 5) – Question not referred by a court or tribunal – Jurisdiction
                  of the Court – Manifest inadmissibility.
               
Operative part: 
The Court of Justice of the European Union clearly has no jurisdiction to rule on the question referred by the Município de
         Barcelos.
