
Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of June – Agencja Wydawnicza Technopol v OHIM
(Joined Cases C-54/P and C-55/P)
Appeal – Community trade mark – Refusal to register signs ‘350’, ‘250’, ‘150’, ‘222’, ‘333’ and ‘555’ as trade marks for periodicals, books and game booklets
         – Regulation (EC) No 40/– Article 7(1)(c) – Descriptive character – OHIM obliged to take account of its earlier decision-making practice
      
Community trade mark – Effects of the Community trade mark – Limitations – Article 12(b) of Regulation No 40/– Purpose – No decisive effect on the interpretation of Article 7(1)(c) of the regulation (Council Regulation No 40/94, Arts 7(1)(c)
         and 12(b)) (see para. 33)
      
Re: 
Appeal brought against the judgment of the Court of First Instance (Second Chamber) of November in Joined Cases T-64/to T‑66/and in Joined Cases T-200/to T-202/Agencja Wydawnicza Technopol 
v 
OHIM
, by which that court dismissed the six actions brought against the decisions of the Fourth Board of Appeal of OHIM of December
                  (Cases R 1033/2006-4, R 1034/2006-4, R 1035/2006-4, R 1276/2006‑4, R 1277/2006–and R 1278/2006-4) concerning applications
                  for registration of the word marks 150, 250, 350, 222, and as Community trade marks – Infringement of Articles 7(1)(c)
                  and of Council Regulation (EC) No 40/of December on the Community trade mark (OJ L 11, p. 1), as replaced
                  by Council Regulation (EC) No 207/of February on the Community trade mark (OJ L 78, p. 1) – Descriptive
                  character of marks composed exclusively of figures.
               
Operative part
1.
	The appeals are dismissed. 
2.
	Agencja Wydawnicza Technopol sp. z o.o. is ordered to pay the costs.
