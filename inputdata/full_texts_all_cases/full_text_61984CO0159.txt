Grounds
BY APPLICATIONS LODGED AT THE COURT REGISTRY ON JUNE 1984, NOVEMBER 1984, JANUARY AND AUGUST ALAN AINSWORTH AND OTHERS BROUGHT ACTIONS UNDER ARTICLE OF THE EAEC TREATY AND ARTICLE OF THE STAFF REGULATIONS FOR :  ( I ) THE ANNULMENT OF THE DECISION BY WHICH THE DIRECTOR OF THE JOINT EUROPEAN TORUS ( JET ) JOINT UNDERTAKING REFUSED TO ENGAGE THE APPLICANTS AS TEMPORARY SERVANTS OF THE COMMISSION OF THE EAEC;  ( II ) IN THE ALTERNATIVE, A DECLARATION THAT THE COMMISSION HAS INFRINGED THE PROVISIONS OF THE EAEC TREATY BY FAILING TO ADDRESS TO THE APPLICANTS AN OFFER OF EMPLOYMENT AS TEMPORARY SERVANTS;  ( III ) A DECLARATION THAT THE COMMUNITY ( EAEC OR EEC ) IS LIABLE TO COMPENSATE THE APPLICANTS FOR THE LOSS SUFFERED BY REASON OF THE UNLAWFUL RECRUITMENT PROCEDURES ADOPTED BY THE COUNCIL AND IMPLEMENTED BY THE COMMISSION .  THOSE APPLICATIONS WERE PRECEDED BY ACTIONS BROUGHT BY THE SAME APPLICANTS UNDER THE SECOND PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE 146, THE THIRD PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE 148, ARTICLE AND THE SECOND PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE OF THE EAEC TREATY AND ARTICLE AND THE SECOND PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE OF THE EEC TREATY . THOSE ACTIONS WERE DECLARED ADMISSIBLE AND GAVE RISE, ON THEIR SUBSTANCE, TO THE JUDGMENT OF JANUARY IN JOINED CASES 271/83, 15, 36, 113, 158, 203/AND 13/AINSWORTH AND OTHERS V COMMISSION AND COUNCIL (( )) ECR ).  IN THOSE CIRCUMSTANCES, IT IS TO BE OBSERVED THAT THE ACTIONS IN CASES AND 267/84, AND 264/85, WHICH WERE BROUGHT AT A LATER DATE, ARE BETWEEN THE SAME PARTIES, HAVE THE SAME PURPOSE AND ARE BASED ON THE SAME SUBMISSIONS AS THE ACTIONS WHICH LED TO THE JUDGMENT OF JANUARY IN JOINED CASES 271/83, 15, 36, 113, 203/AND 13/AINSWORTH AND OTHERS V COMMISSION AND COUNCIL .  THE ACTIONS IN CASES AND 267/84, AND 264/MUST THEREFORE BE DISMISSED AS INADMISSIBLE PURSUANT TO ARTICLE 92*() OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE .  
Decision on costs
COSTS  UNDER ARTICLE 69*() OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE THE UNSUCCESSFUL PARTY IS TO BE ORDERED TO PAY THE COSTS . HOWEVER, UNDER ARTICLE OF THOSE RULES, COSTS INCURRED BY THE INSTITUTIONS IN PROCEEDINGS BROUGHT BY SERVANTS OF THE COMMUNITIES ARE TO BE BORNE BY THE INSTITUTIONS .  
Operative part
ON THOSE GROUNDS,  THE COURT ( THIRD CHAMBER )  HEREBY ORDERS AS FOLLOWS :  ( ) THE APPLICATIONS ARE DISMISSED AS INADMISSIBLE;  ( ) THE PARTIES ARE ORDERED TO BEAR THEIR OWN COSTS .  LUXEMBOURG, APRIL .
