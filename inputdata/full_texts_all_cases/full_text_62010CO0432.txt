
Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of January – Chihabi and Others v Geert Vandendriessche, acting in his capacity
            as receiver of Anneessen BVBA
(Case C-432/10)
Reference for a preliminary ruling – Manifest inadmissibility
Preliminary rulings – Admissibility – Questions referred without sufficient information on the factual and legislative context (Art. TFEU; Statute of the Court
         of Justice, Art. 23; Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice, Arts 92(1) and 103(1)) (see paras 18-23)
      
Re:
Reference for a preliminary ruling – Rechtbank van eerste aanleg te Antwerpen – Interpretation of Articles 5, to and
                  43, second indent of Article and Articles 202(1) and (3) and 221(1) and (3) of Council Regulation (EEC) No 2913/of
                  October establishing the Community Customs Code (OJ L 302, p. 1) and of Article 199(1) of Commission Regulation
                  (EEC) No 2454/of July laying down provisions for the implementation of Council Regulation (EEC) No 2913/establishing
                  the Community Customs Code (OJ L 253, p. 1) – Post-clearance recovery of import or export duties – Communication to the
                  debtor – Creation of a customs debt following the unlawful introduction of goods.
               
Operative part
 
	The reference for a preliminary ruling from the Rechtbank van eerste aanleg te Antwerpen, made by decision of May 2007,
                     is manifestly inadmissible.
