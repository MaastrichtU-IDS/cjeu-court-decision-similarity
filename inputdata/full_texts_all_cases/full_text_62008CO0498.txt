
Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of July – Fornaci Laterizi Danesi v Commission
(Case C‑498/P)
Appeal – Action for annulment – Time-limit for bringing an action – Point from which time starts to run – Inadmissibility because out of time – Appeal manifestly unfounded
Actions for annulment – Time-limits – Point from which time starts to run – Day on which a measure came to the knowledge of the applicant – Subsidiary matter (Art. 230, fifth para., EC) (see paras 21-22, 27)
Re: 
Appeal brought against the order of the Court of First Instance (First Chamber) of September in Case T-224/Fornaci Laterizi Danesi
 v 
Commission
 by which that court dismissed as inadmissible the application for annulment of Commission Decision 2008/25/EC of November
                  adopting, pursuant to Council Directive 92/43/EEC, a first updated list of sites of Community importance for the Continental
                  biogeographical region (OJ L 12, p. 383), in so far as a plot of land belonging to the applicant, under reference IT20A0018,
                  was included in that list.
               
Operative part: 
1.
	The appeal is dismissed.
2.
	Fornaci Laterizi Danesi SpA is ordered to pay the costs.
