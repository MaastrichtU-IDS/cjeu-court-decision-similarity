++++IN CASE 133/HERBERT BRUNS, APPLICANT,  V  COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, DEFENDANT,  
Grounds
WHEREAS BY A STATEMENT LODGED AT THE REGISTRY ON MAY THE APPLICANT DECLARED THAT HE WITHDREW HIS APPLICATION SINCE AN ARRANGEMENT HAD BEEN REACHED BETWEEN THE PARTIES ON THE PRINCIPLE, THE AMOUNTS AND THE COSTS;  WHEREAS BY A STATEMENT LODGED AT THE REGISTRY ON MAY THE DEFENDANT STATED THAT IT AGREED TO THIS WITHDRAWAL;  
Operative part
CASE 133/IS REMOVED FROM THE REGISTER OF THE COURT .
