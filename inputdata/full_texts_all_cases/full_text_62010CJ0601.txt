
Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of October – Commission v Greece
(Case C-601/10)
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Directives 92/50/EEC and 2004/18/EC – Public service contracts – Additional land registry and town planning services – Negotiated procedure without prior publication of a contract notice
1.                     Actions for failure to fulfil obligations – Examination of the merits by the Court – Acknowledgement of its failure by the
            Member State concerned – No effect (Art. TFEU) (see para. 28)
2.                     Approximation of laws – Procedures for the award of public service contracts – Directives 92/and 2004/– Derogations
            from common rules – Restrictive interpretation – Existence of exceptional circumstances – Burden of proof (European Parliament
            and Council Directive 2004/18, Art. 31(4)(a); Council Directive 92/50, Art. 11(3)(e)) (see paras 32-33)
3.                     Actions for failure to fulfil obligations – Examination of the merits by the Court – Situation to be taken into consideration
            – Situation on expiry of the period laid down in the reasoned opinion (Art. TFEU) (see para. 40)
4.                     Member States – Obligations – Implementation of directives – Failure to fulfil obligations – National system pleaded as justification
            – Not permissible (Art. TFEU) (see para. 41)
Re
Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Infringement of Articles and 11(3) of Council Directive 92/50/EEC of June relating to the coordination of procedures for the award of public service contracts (OJ L 209, p. 1) and of
                  Articles and 31(4) of Directive 2004/18/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of March on the coordination
                  of procedures for the award of public works contracts, public supply contracts and public service contracts (OJ L 134,
                  p. 114) – Award of a contract without prior publication of a contract notice – Contract relating to additional land-registry
                  and town-planning services – Municipalities of Vasilika, Kassandra, Egnatia and Arethousa.
               
Operative part
The Court: 
1.
	Declares that, by awarding, by means of a negotiated procedure without prior publication of a contract notice, public contracts
                     for additional land registry and town planning services which do not appear in the initial contract concluded by the municipalities
                     of Vasilika, Kassandra, Egnatia and Arethousa, the Hellenic Republic has failed to fulfil its obligations under Articles and 11(3) of Council Directive 92/50/EEC of June relating to the coordination of procedures for the award of public
                     service contracts, as amended by Directive 97/52/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of October 1997, and
                     Articles and 31(4) of Directive 2004/18/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of March on the coordination
                     of procedures for the award of public works contracts, public supply contracts and public service contracts; 
                  
2.
	Orders the Hellenic Republic to pay the costs.
