
Order of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of December – González Alonso v Nationale Nederlanden Vida Cia De Seguros y Reaseguros
(Case C‑352/10)
Reference for a preliminary ruling – Inadmissibility
Questions referred for a preliminary ruling – Admissibility – Questions referred without enough information on the factual and legislative context – Manifest inadmissibility (Art. TFEU; Statute of the Court of Justice, Art. 23; Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice,
         Arts 92(1) and 103(1)) (see paras 9-12, 18, operative part)
      
Re: 
Reference for a preliminary ruling – Audiencia Provincial de Oviedo – Interpretation of Article 3(2)(d) of Council Directive
                  85/577/EEC of December to protect the consumer in respect of contracts negotiated away from business premises (OJ L 372, p. 31) – Contract, concluded away from business premises, under which life assurance is offered in return for payment
                  of a monthly premium invested in various products of the company itself.
               
Operative part
The reference for a preliminary ruling made by the Audiencia Provincial de Oviedo by decision of June is manifestly
         inadmissible.
