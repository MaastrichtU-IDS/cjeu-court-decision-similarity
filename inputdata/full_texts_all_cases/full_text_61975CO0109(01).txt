IN JOINED CASES AND 114/NATIONAL CARBONISING COMPANY LIMITED , MANSFIELD  AGAINST  COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES  NATIONAL COAL BOARD , INTERVENING  UNITED KINGDOM , INTERVENING  
Grounds
BY LETTER OF JANUARY THE APPLICANT ASKS TO BE ALLOWED TO WITHDRAW ITS APPLICATIONS TO THE COURT . THIS REQUEST HAS BEEN SERVED ON THE DEFENDANT AND THE INTERVENING PARTIES WHO HAVE INFORMED THE COURT THAT THEY HAVE NO OBJECTION TO SUCH WITHDRAWAL . NONE OF THE PARTIES SEEK AN ORDER AS TO COSTS .  PURSUANT THEREFORE TO ARTICLE OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE OF THE COURT THE COURT SHOULD ORDER THAT JOINED CASES AND 114/BE REMOVED FROM THE COURT REGISTER .
